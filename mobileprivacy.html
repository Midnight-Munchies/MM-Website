1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) from users of the Game.

2. Third-Party Services

The Game uses third-party services to provide features like advertisements, leaderboards, and achievements. These services may collect, process, and store data as described in their respective privacy policies. Below are the details of these services:

2.1 ironSource

The Game uses ironSource as a mediation platform to display advertisements. ironSource works with various ad networks and may collect data such as device identifiers, IP addresses, and app usage data to deliver ads and optimize ad performance.

For more information about ironSource's privacy practices, please refer to their Privacy Policy: https://www.is.com/privacy-policy/.

You can also review ironSource's partner ad networks and their respective privacy policies via their partner list: https://developers.is.com/ironsource-mobile/general/mediated-network-partners/.

2.2 Google Play Games Services

The Game uses Google Play Games Services for login, leaderboards, and achievements on Android devices. These services may collect user data such as Google Account information and gameplay statistics.

For more information about Google Play Games Services' privacy practices, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

2.3 Apple Game Center

The Game uses Apple Game Center for login, leaderboards, and achievements on iOS devices. These services may collect user data such as Apple ID information and gameplay statistics.

For more information about Apple Game Center's privacy practices, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

3. Children's Privacy

Our Game is designed for players of all ages, including children under the age of 13. To comply with applicable privacy laws such as COPPA and GDPR for children, we do not knowingly collect, store, or process any personal information from children.

However, third-party services used in the Game (such as ironSource, Google Play Games Services, and Apple Game Center) may collect limited data, such as device identifiers, to provide their services. These third parties are responsible for ensuring their compliance with children's privacy laws. For details about their practices, please refer to their privacy policies linked in Section 2.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information to us without your consent, please contact us at the email address provided below, and we will take steps to delete such information.

You can manage or restrict advertising-related data collection by reviewing your device settings or the privacy settings provided by ironSource and its partners.

Similarly, you can manage the data shared with Google Play Games Services and Apple Game Center by reviewing the privacy settings in your respective accounts.
